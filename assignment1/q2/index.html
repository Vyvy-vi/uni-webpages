About

This page consists of some of my most favorite comic strips from XKCD's WebComic. I really enjoy scrolling through the comics on that page, whenever I'm free. XKCD describes itself as "A webcomic of romance, sarcasm, math, and language". I wish more people in our university talked about interesting websites, web comics and blogs like this one. I usually only get to talk about cool things like this with friends I made online...
